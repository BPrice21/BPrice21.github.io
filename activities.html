Alpha Gamma Sigma

Alpha Chapter at The Ohio State University

Activities

AGS competes in Ohio State's intramural sports programs, fielding teams in football, basketball, and softball. AGS regularly beats AGR when AGR can field a team.

The fraternity members work with Saddle & Sirloin Club to host the Scarlet & Gray Midwest Showdown Steer Show each winter. This show benefits Ohio agriculture, gives the members an important project on which to work together, and is a major fundraiser for AGS.

AGS also takes an active role with many clubs including:

  • Saddle & Sirloin
  • Ohio State University Livestock Judging Team
  • Buckeye Dairy Club
  • Green Engineering Scholars
  • Agricultural Systems Management Club
