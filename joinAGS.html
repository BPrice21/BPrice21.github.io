Alpha Gamma Sigma

Alpha Chapter at The Ohio State University

Scholars and Leaders

As a professional agricultural fraternity, Alpha Gamma Sigma is always looking for new members that strive to be leaders and scholars on campus and beyond. No matter your major, AGS fosters an environment for you to excel in scholarship, leadership and service.

How to Get Involved?

Alpha Gamma Sigma hosts and participates in many events throughout the school year. To find out more, check out our event calendar below or contact our Recruitment Chairman Zak Jones (jones.6246@osu.edu).
